Mean (The Average)

  • Sum up every number in the dataset.
  • Divide that total sum by the count of items in the set.
Set: [3, 8, 4] → Sum = 15
Divide: 15 ÷ 3 = Mean of 5

Median (The Middle)

  • Sort all items from least to greatest first!
  • Odd Count: Grab the exact single middle number.
  • Even Count: Take the mean (average) of the two middle numbers.
Odd Set: [2, 5, 9]
→ Middle is Median of 5
Even Set: [1, 3, 5, 9] (Middle are 3 and 5)
→ (3 + 5) ÷ 2 = Median of 4

Mode (The Frequency)

  • Identify the value that appears most frequently.
  • Bimodal / Multimodal: If multiple numbers tie for the highest count, they are all modes.
  • No Mode: If every single value appears exactly the same number of times.
Standard Mode: [4, 9, 9, 2] → Mode: 9
Multiple Modes: [1, 1, 6, 7, 7] → Modes: 1 and 7
No Mode: [3, 5, 8, 11] → No Mode
